## 🛒 Ingredients from Swedish Suppliers

### 🍺 Malt / Extract Base

To replicate a balanced pale base (light, simple and fermentable):

**Option A — Simple extract approach**  
- LME/DME isn’t very common in Swedish stores — you *could* sub with a small kit + adjunct grains.  
- If you already have a Munich or Pale Ale extract, use it here.

**Option B — Partial grain (easier to source)**  
- **BEST Pale Ale 10 kg** (Pale Ale Malt) — primary fermentables.  
  • Use ~3.5 – 4.5 kg for ~20–23 L batch.  
  • You can add ~0.5–1 kg flaked oats or wheat from Mr-Malt to mimic haze and texture.  

&gt; If you want convenience and haze from grains, go with a **partial grain** approach (like many NEIPA kits use oats/wheat). Alternatively, use an extract kit like the MR-Malt *Mosaic IPA kit* below as the base.

### 🍺 Complete Kits (Extract + Hops) — Simplest Alternative

- **Mosaic IPA receptkit 20 l** — This is a *ready extract kit* with Citra/Mosaic hops which gives a juicy, hazy-leaning IPA flavour.  
  • Comes with malt extract, hops, and yeast.  
  • Good *base* for a hazy pale ale — you can tweak with extra late hops/torrhumle (dry hop) for more haze &amp; aroma.

---

### 🌾 Additional Grains for Haze/Body
*(If doing partial grain instead of full extract)*  
- Flaked oats (malt/grain for haze &amp; mouthfeel) — available from Mr-Malt (search “Flaked Oats”).  
- Wheat malt — also available on Mr-Malt/Olbryggning.

---

### 🌿 Hops (for aroma &amp; haze)
You want **fruity, aromatic hops** (similar to Falconer’s Flight + Nectaron / Superdelic style):  
- Citra — classic citrus/fruity.  
- Mosaic — complex tropical fruit.  
- Galaxy or Nectaron if available (check stock).  

➡ These are available both as individual hop pellets on **Mr-Malt.se** and in some kits.  

**Hopping suggestions:**  
• Add a *late addition* (~10 min from end of boil) of Citra + Mosaic for aroma.  
• Do **dry hopping** after fermentation (big part of hazy character).  

---

### 🍺 Yeast
For haze and juicy character, use a **New England / Hazy ale yeast**:  
- Mr-Malt carries NEIPA yeast strains (Pomona, Verdant, etc.) on their recipe kits page. ([Mr. Malt](https://mr-malt.se/olrecept?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

## 🛠️ Simplified Brew Plan (≈20–23 L)

**Option 1 — Using Mosaic IPA Extract Kit** (easiest)  
1. Brew the **Mosaic IPA kit** per instructions.  
2. Boost with extra **dry hops** (Citra, Mosaic) late/after fermentation for haze and fruitiness.  
3. Ferment with a NEIPA yeast strain.

**Option 2 — Partial Grain Hazy Pale Ale**  
1. Mash (single infusion @ ~66–68 °C, 60 min):  
   - ~4.0 kg BEST Pale Ale Malt  
   - ~0.7–1.0 kg flaked oats (for haze &amp; body)  
   - Optional ~0.2–0.4 kg wheat malt  
2. Boil ~60 min (can skip long boil if doing BIAB/extract style):  
   - Add bittering hops (small amount) early  
   - Add aroma hops (Citra/Mosaic) 10–15 min from end  
3. Chill, ferment with NEIPA yeast  
4. Dry hop generously after primary fermentation (~3–7 d)  
5. Carbonate &amp; enjoy
