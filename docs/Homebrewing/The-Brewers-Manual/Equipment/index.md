# Equipment guides

These guides apply the materials and chemistry framework from [The Brewer's Manual](../01-introduction.md) to specific pieces of equipment — covering per-component material analysis, compatibility ratings, cleaning sequences, and known failure modes.

Each guide cross-references back to the relevant material pages in the [materials register](../05-materials-register/index.md) and to the [wet-dry cycle model](../04-wdc-model.md) for the underlying reasoning.

- [Oxebar 4 L mini keg](./03-oxebar-mini-keg.md) — multilayer PET; Santoprene elbow; CO₂ purge sanitisation
- [Bottles and bottling](./04-bottles-and-bottling.md) — PET and glass bottles; crown cap liners; oxygen management

- [Serving systems](./06-serving-systems.md) — EVABarrier line; POK DuoTight fittings; NukaTap
- [Cask dispense](./07-cask-dispense.md) — spiles, keystones, taps; sustained wet storage considerations
