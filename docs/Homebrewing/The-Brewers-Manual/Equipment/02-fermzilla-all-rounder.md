# FermZilla All Rounder (30 L)

:::note Status
**Stub** — content to be written.
:::

**Covers:** The KegLand FermZilla All Rounder 30 L pressure-capable PET fermenter. Note: a 20 L FermZilla does not exist. The 20 L fermentation option in the BrewClub context is the Witre PP bucket — see [bucket fermenter guide](./01-bucket-fermenter.md).

**Components:**
- Vessel: PET (clear)
- Lid ring, base, handles: GR engineering plastic — inferred GR-POK (KegLand "GR" designation); EU food contact confirmation pending from KegLand EU
- Lid O-ring: LOW2 (brown, post-2023 units) — pre-2023 black O-rings were silicone, not LOW2; check colour before assuming
- BlowTie spunding valve diaphragm: material confirmation pending from KegLand
- Collection container (Tri-Conical variant): Tritan co-polyester, BPA-free

**Key findings:**
- StellarSan storage: confirmed safe by KegLand — leave-in indefinitely. US retailer "one hour" guidance was incorrect.
- LOW2 EU food contact status: pending KegLand EU confirmation
- Pressure vessel service life: inspect lid O-ring and lid ring at each brew; pressure capability is a function of seal integrity
- Cleaning constraints: no abrasive cleaning inside PET vessel; PBW soak appropriate; brush threads and fittings
