# Oxebar 4 L mini keg

:::note Status
**Stub** — content to be written.
:::

**Covers:** The Oxebar 4 L multilayer PET mini keg — construction, compatibility, sanitisation method, temperature limits, and serving.

**Construction:**
- Multilayer PET with oxygen barrier layers — significantly lower OTR than plain PET
- Gen 2 elbow: Santoprene (TPV — thermoplastic vulcanisate) — confirmed by KegLand
- Thread seals: PTFE tape

**Sanitisation method:**
- CO₂ purge method with ChemiPro DES: spray interior, close, shake, vent CO₂ while holding upside down to drain
- DES is the preferred sanitiser for Oxebar — ethanol-based, no non-volatile residue, *A*-rated for PET
- ABNS is usable but requires thorough drainage; DES preferred for simplicity and zero WDC risk

**Temperature limits:**
- Critical — do not exceed rated filling temperature
- PET softens at elevated temperatures; multilayer construction does not change this
- Fill with cooled, fermented beer only — never hot wort

**Oxygen management:**
- Multilayer construction provides superior O₂ barrier vs. plain PET bottles
- CO₂ purge before filling eliminates headspace oxygen — key advantage over gravity bottling

**Serving:**
- Picnic tap (basic): functional, inexpensive, suitable for BrewClub and casual use
- Natural carbonation with carbonation drops: no CO₂ equipment required
- Spunding valve option: natural carbonation with pressure control
- Forced carbonation: requires CO₂ cylinder and regulator
