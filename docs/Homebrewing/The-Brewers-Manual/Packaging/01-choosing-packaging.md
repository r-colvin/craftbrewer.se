# Choosing a packaging option

*This page is a stub. Content is planned.*

Packaging choice affects oxygen ingress, shelf life, carbonation control, and practical convenience. The key options for homebrewers are:

- Standard PET bottles (bottle-conditioned or force-carbonated)
- Oxebar barrier PET kegs (Mono/Monolayer)
- Glass bottles
- Stainless steel kegs (Cornelius, mini kegs)
- Cans (out of scope for most homebrewers)

A full comparison by oxygen transmission rate, style suitability, storage duration, and cost is planned here.

See also the oxygen ingress comparison table on the [PET materials page](../05-materials-register/03-pet.md#oxygen-ingress).
