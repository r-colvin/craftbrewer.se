BrewClub is a guided brewing programme for new brewers. It takes you from your first brew through progressively more advanced techniques in a linear, step-by-step format. Each stage links into [The Brewer's Manual](../The-Brewers-Manual/01-introduction.md) when you want to understand the science behind the recommendation.

The programme is designed so that every stage can be exited at any point — you are never committed to buying the next piece of equipment before you are ready.

## The brew sequence

- **Brew 1 — Fresh wort kit:** A pre-made wort split across 4–5 people. Everyone chooses their own yeast and dry hop addition; everyone gets a unique beer from the same base. The lowest-friction possible introduction to brewing.
- **Brew 2 — Cider:** Introduces yeast nutrient, back-sweetening, and adjuncts (apple pie cider, dry-hopped cider, and so on). Interchangeable with Brew 1.
- **Brew 3 — Partial mash / extract:** First custom wort. Specialty grain steeping, hop additions, partial boil option for small pots.
- **Brew 4 — All-grain stove-top:** Full mash on the hob. No new equipment required beyond Brew 3.
