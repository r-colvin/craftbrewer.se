# Serving systems

**Covers:** Beer line, fittings, taps, and the complete serving system from keg post to glass.

**Beer line — EVABarrier:**
- Co-extruded multilayer line; significantly lower O₂ transmission than standard vinyl tubing
- Food contact compliant
- Line length and diameter: balancing carbonation pressure against pour rate — calculation explained
- Cleaning: flow-through PBW then rinse; no disassembly required for routine cleaning

**Fittings — POK DuoTight:**
- KegLand's replacement for POM collar DuoTight — POK (polyketone) rated A for ABNS and DES
- John Guest fittings: POM body — still present in older systems; check for cracking under ESC conditions
- Ball lock posts: stainless or POK internals

**Taps — NukaTap:**
- Stainless faceplate
- POK internals (post-redesign)
- Nozzle exterior: sanitise by DES spray before each use; residual beer on exterior is the primary contamination source

**Line cleaning frequency:**
- Commercial standard: every two weeks for active taps
- Homebrew reality: clean between each keg change as minimum
- Procedure: PBW flow-through, water rinse, optional ABNS fill for storage
