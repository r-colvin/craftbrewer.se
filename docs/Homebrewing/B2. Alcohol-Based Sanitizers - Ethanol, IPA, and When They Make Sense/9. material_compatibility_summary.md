title: "Material compatibility summary"
---

| Material | Alcohol compatibility |
|---|---|
| PP | Excellent |
| HDPE | Excellent |
| PET | Good |
| POK | Excellent |
| Silicone | Good (short contact) |
| EPDM | Good |
| NBR | Moderate |
| PC | Poor–moderate |

Short contact is key — alcohol soaking elastomers provides no benefit.
