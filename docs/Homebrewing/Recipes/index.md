A shared recipe collection designed around the BrewClub equipment list and scalable to larger setups. Recipes are organised by method rather than style — the same pale ale can be brewed as a fresh wort kit split, an extract brew, or an all-grain recipe depending on where you are in the BrewClub journey.

## Collections

- [Fresh wort kits](./fwk/index.md) — yeast choices, dry hop additions, and split variations
- [Extract and partial mash](./extract/index.md) — recipes using malt extract with optional specialty grain steeping
- [All-grain](./all-grain/index.md) — full mash recipes for stove-top and larger systems
