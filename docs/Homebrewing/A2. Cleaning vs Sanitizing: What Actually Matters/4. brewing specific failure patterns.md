## The brewing-specific failure pattern

In brewing, failures typically follow this sequence:

1. Incomplete cleaning leaves thin organic films  
2. Sanitizer is applied successfully — at first  
3. Residues accumulate in crevices and interfaces  
4. Sanitizer efficacy declines silently  
5. Infection appears “out of nowhere”  

This pattern explains why anecdotal evidence is unreliable: early success masks the underlying failure mode.
