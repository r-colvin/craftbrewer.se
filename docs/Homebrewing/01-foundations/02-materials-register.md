# Materials register

:::note Status
**Stub** — content to be written.
:::

**Covers:** Every material found in common homebrewing equipment — polymers, elastomers, metals, glass — rated against the chemicals used in brewing (ABNS, DES, PBW, StellarOxy). Traffic-light rating system: A (compatible), B (use with caution), C (limited use), D (avoid), X (incompatible).

**Materials to cover:**
- PP (polypropylene)
- HDPE
- PET / Oxebar multilayer
- POK (polyketone)
- POM (acetal) — legacy and current
- PC (polycarbonate) — legacy
- PS (polystyrene) — airlock context
- SAN
- Nylon 12
- EPDM
- Silicone (platinum-cured vs. peroxide-cured)
- NR (natural rubber) — grommet context
- SBR
- Viton (peroxide-cured vs. polyamine-cured)
- EVA (EVABarrier)
- Stainless steel 304/316
- Glass
- Crown cap liners
